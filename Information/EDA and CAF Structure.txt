Composition of EDA
EDA will consist of seven Departments, as follows:
(1). Chief Executive Department
This is the coordinating office of EDA. 
Its functions include:
• Supervision of the day-to-day activities of all the departments of EDA. 
• The official spokesperson of EDA 
• Ensures the vision of EDA is adhered to by all the Departments
• Staff regular training to conform with EDA’s vision
• Calling up of meetings and making arrangements for the success of each meeting.
• Record keeping of each meeting.
The Department will have a Secretary, who will be the official Secretary of EDA. The job of the secretary is to perform the functions of the CEO office under the leadership of the CEO.
The leader of this Department will be known as the Chief Executive Officer (CEO).

(2). Financial Department
Its functions include:
• In charge of day-to-day running of financial activities of EDA
• Oversees the financial execution of Eda projects
• Interpret the budgetary allocation for the running of EDA
• Advise the Board of Eda Foundation on financial matters
• Keep proper records of Eda financial activities.
This Department will be made up of accountants, HR personnel, etc.
The leader of the Financial Department will be known as the Financial Head.

(3). Legal Department
This department is in charge of all legal matters of EDA. 
The functions include:
• Review and advise us on all legal issues.
• Check all the documentations to ascertain their EDA’s legal compliance.
• Guide, prepare, and ratify any appropriate documents for any contractual relationship that EDA will want to enter into.
• Attend to all our legal issues of EDA.
This Department will be made up of legal personnel.
The leader of the department will be known as the Legal Head.

(4). Logistic Department
This department is in charge of business development. 
Its works is to “think” out the way forward for EDA. 
Its functions include:
• Formulate new project ideas in line with EDA’s vision through creative and innovative ingenuity
• Develop idea into a digital design for approval by the Board of Eda Foundation
• Design the structure of Eda Ecosystem
• Design how each project in Eda Ecosystem will interact with all the other projects to advance EDA’s vision.
• Identify the need for staff recruitment for all the Department and make such recommendations to the Board for approval.
• Organize employment interviews along with the relevant Department and the Department of Finance. In other words, those that will sit in each employment interview are personnel from the Logistic Department, Financial Department, and the relevant Department in which the employee will be working. 
Thus, the work of this Department is to “think” out the way forward for EDA. Therefore, the Department will consist of individuals with brilliant minds whose job is principally to produce creative and innovative solutions in line with EDA’s vision.
The leader of this Department will be known as the Logistic Head.

(5). Marketing Department
This department is in charge of marketing Eda projects. 
Its function include:
• Using all legal means to advertise and promote EDA’s projects
• Searching for partnerships and make recommendations for their endorsement by the Board.
This Department will be made up of marketers.
The leader will be known as the Marketing Head.

(6). Statistics Department
This department will be in charge of data management. 
Its function include:
• Collate, arrange and coordinate information into statistics for usage in advancing the vision of EDA. 
• Record keeping of EDA’s data
• Periodical release of data to show the progress of EDA in line with the vision
This Department will be made up of statisticians and data managers.
The leader will be known as the Statistics Head.

(7). Eda Labs
This is in charge of the practical implementation or bringing EDA’s images into physical reality. 

Functions of EDA Lab include:
• Design, build, and maintain the EDA project platform
• Can also generate creative and innovative ideas for Eda Ecosystem.
EDA Labs will be a leader in building distributed computing systems and decentralized technology solutions.
EDA Labs will be committed to open-source principles, and ethical, purpose-driven business, creating technology to benefit its users in line with the vision of EDA. 
Thus, whereas the Logistic Department will generate ideas, Eda Labs will be in charge of bringing such ideas into physical reality. 
Eda Labs will be comprised of dynamic, innovative IT personnel – based all over the world, collectively committed to innovation through delivering the highest standards in software engineering based on rigorous peer-to-peer reviewed science.
The leader will be known as the Technological Head.


Article 6.0.0. Disengagement
-	Any member of the Project Team can pull out from the Project Team but should be done honorably, giving at least one month of notice.
-	Any member of the Project Team can be removed from the Team by the CEO if such a member:
(a). shows lack of interest
(b). under-performing despite adequate attempts to remedy the situation
(c). commits an act that retards the progress of the Project
(d). have legal issues that will be detrimental to the progress of the Project

The BOF of EDA will serve the following functions:
• Eda BOF’s most crucial responsibility will be to drive Eda ecosystem adoption and promoting and protecting the vision of EDA.
• Eda BOF will be involved on promoting the academic study of blockchain and pioneer digitalization of Africa in collaboration with other key holders.
• Eda BOF will be dedicated to facilitating blockchain education – offering courses and expertise to institutions, professionals, enterprises, and blockchain beginners within Africa and beyond.
• Eda BOF will be committed to working with other key industries within the blockchain realm, contributing to the advancement of the technology and driving adoption with other compatible systems. 
• The BOF will contribute to the blockchain technology by focusing on how to utilize the technology to advance healthcare provision, banking services, legal frameworks, with the ultimate goal to bringing new insight for the financial freedom of many Africans and beyond.
• Approves projects submitted by any of the seven Departments
• Draw up EDA’s budget that will be finally endorsed by the Director

• Research works on emerging digital world such as the Crypto Market, e-Medicine, etc.
• Maintain discipline among EDA staff
• Publication of research studies for rapid adoption of cryptos and the various aspects of digitalization.
Thus, the functions of BOF is to promote, protect, and preserve the vision of EDA. Therefore, the slogan of BOF is: Promote, Protect and Preserve EDA.

Office of the Director of EDA
This is the Office of the Founding Director. 
It is the highest-ranking office of EDA.
The Office is in charge of formulating EDA’s vision.
The office will be in charge of approval of all budgetary recommendations of the Board of Founders.
The Director’s office is a lifetime office.
The Director approves the appointment of the CEO by the Board of Founders.
The Director has the right to terminate the appointment of the CEO after due consultation with members of the Board of Founders.
The Director has the sole right to appoint anyone to join him as a co-Director of EDA or to replace him as the Director in the event he retires.
In the event of demise, the able ward (child or family member) of the Director assumes the Director’s Office without any contest and no recourse to any approval from any person, staff, or office. 
In case EDA becomes a public limited company, the Director will have the majority of the shares with controlling advantage, which means a minimum of 50% of total shares.

Remuneration and Benefits for Members of BOF
There are three benefits a member of BOF will get:
(1). Governance rights for EdaFace
A member of BOF has decision-making right on the development of EdaFace and Eda Ecosystem. 

(2). Sharing in Eda Token
18 to 20% of Eda Token total supply will be allocated to the members of BOF and other members of the Project Team.
The share of each member in the allocated Eda Token will be depended on his/her contributions to the Project development as follows:
(a). Project idea development (50% of Project Team token allocation, which is 10 trillion EDA)
(b). Financial contribution (20% of Project Team token allocation, which is 4 trillion EDA)
(c). IT construction contribution (20% of Project Team token allocation, which is 4 trillion EDA)
(d). Department building Contribution (10% of Project Team token allocation, which is 2 trillion EDA).

(3). Share in the EdaFace financial generations
EdaFace is built to generate continuous income from its services. 
Each member of BOF will share in EdaFace income generation.
The share each member will get depend on the member’s contributions.

Modalities of sharing will be worked out as the Project develops.
Tentatively, the following will be the modalities for sharing:
30% goes to the BOF to be shared to each member and payment of salaries
20% goes to Eda Lab – for continuous innovation and creativity
30% will be set aside as EDA’s savings and investments
10% for miscellaneous usage
10% goes to CAF – this will help to reduce tax pressure on EDA.
